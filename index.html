Privacy Policy for King James Bible

Last Updated: July 29, 2025

King James Bible ("the App"), developed by JoshTechApps, is a free, ad-supported application that provides daily Bible verses, prayers, quizzes, and customization features. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Data We Collect

  • Advertising Data: The App uses Google AdMob to display ads (Interstitial, Native Advanced, Rewarded). AdMob may collect device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads.
  • Local Data: Notes, favorite verses, and quiz progress are stored locally on your device using Room persistence. This data is not synced or shared.
  • Notifications: If enabled, the App uses device permissions to schedule morning (08:00) and night (21:00) notifications for verses and prayers.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect personal details like name, email, or location unless provided voluntarily (e.g., via contact).

2. How We Use Data

  • Advertising Data: Used by AdMob to deliver relevant ads and improve ad performance.
  • Local Data: Used to provide features like saving notes, favorite verses, and tracking quiz progress.
  • Notifications: Used to deliver scheduled reminders if opted in via settings.

3. Data Sharing

AdMob may share device identifiers with its advertising partners. See Google’s Privacy Policy for details. Local data and notification preferences remain on your device and are not shared.

4. Your Choices

You can:

  • Reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your device’s settings.
  • Disable notifications in the App’s settings or device settings.
  • Delete local data by clearing the App’s data or uninstalling it.

5. Data Retention

Local data is retained on your device until you clear the App’s data or uninstall it. We do not retain any personal data on our servers.

6. Children

The App is rated E for Everyone and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately. Contact us if you have concerns.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect local data stored on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Check this page for the latest version. Continued use of the App implies consent to updates.

9. Contact Us

For questions, contact us at contactjoshtech@gmail.com.
