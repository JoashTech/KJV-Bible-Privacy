2. How Your Information Is Used

  • Advertising: Our partners use shared device identifiers to display relevant ads and support our free app
  • Analytics: We use shared usage data to understand app performance and improve user experience
  • App Features: To save your notes, favorites, and quiz progress locally on your device
  • Notifications: To deliver scheduled reminders when you opt-in

3. Data Sharing & Transparency

We share device identifiers with advertising and analytics partners. We do NOT share your local data (notes, favorites, quiz progress) or personal information. Third-party partners may process shared device data as outlined in their respective privacy policies.

4. Your Control & Choices

  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Android Settings > Google > Ads)
  • Disable notifications in the app or device settings
  • Delete all local data by clearing app data or uninstalling
  • Reset your Advertising ID for a fresh start
  • Manage data collection through our partners' privacy controls

5. Data Security Secure

We leverage Android's built-in security features including app sandboxing. Since no personal data is transmitted to our servers, there's minimal risk of data breaches.

6. Children's Privacy Family Safe

Our app is rated E for Everyone and complies with COPPA. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents can contact us immediately if they have concerns.

7. International Compliance

For EU (GDPR) and California (CCPA) users: Exercise your rights through device settings or advertising network controls. Contact us for assistance with data requests.
